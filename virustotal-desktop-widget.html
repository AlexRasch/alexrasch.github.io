Privacy Policy for VirusTotal Desktop Widget

Thank you for using the VirusTotal Desktop Widget ("the Application"). This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we handle your data when you use the Application.

1. Information Collection and Use

The VirusTotal Desktop Widget does not collect or store any personal information from its users.

2. VirusTotal API Key

While the Application itself does not collect personal information, it offers functionality that relies on the usage of the VirusTotal API. If you choose to provide a VirusTotal API key and use the functions that depend on the VirusTotal API, your use of the API will be subject to VirusTotal's privacy policy. We encourage you to review the VirusTotal privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

3. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the updated Privacy Policy on this page, and the revised date will be indicated at the top. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting and using your information.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the practices of the VirusTotal Desktop Widget, please contact me at Github (AlexRasch)
